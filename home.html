Garnier Whole Blends is a haircare line that offers a range of products formulated with natural ingredients to nourish and care for different hair types and concerns. Each product in the Whole Blends line combines carefully selected ingredients known for their beneficial properties, such as coconut oil, honey, avocado, green tea, and more, to provide specific benefits for various hair needs, such as hydration, repair, volume, or smoothing.





Vaseline Cocoa Radiant haircare products are designed to nourish and moisturize hair, leaving it soft, smooth, and shiny. Infused with cocoa butter, these products help hydrate and condition dry and damaged hair, promoting healthy-looking locks. The Vaseline Cocoa Radiant line includes shampoos, conditioners, and treatments that aim to restore moisture and vitality to the hair, making it more manageable and lustrous.


Streax Hair Serum is a popular hair care product designed to provide nourishment, shine, and manageability to the hair. Formulated with ingredients that help smoothen frizz, add shine, and protect hair from heat styling damage, Streax Hair Serum is known for its lightweight texture that does not weigh down the hair.This serum is suitable for all hair types and is often used to add a glossy finish, tame flyaways, and improve the overall appearance and texture of the hair.





Garnier SkinActive Vitamin C products are skincare essentials infused with Vitamin C, known for its brightening and antioxidant properties. The Vitamin C range includes serums, moisturizers, and treatments that help even out skin tone, reduce signs of dullness, and protect the skin from environmental stressors. These products are designed to revitalize the skin, leaving it looking radiant, refreshed, and more youthful.
